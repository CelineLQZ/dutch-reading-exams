# KNM — Knowledge Points Translation + Vocabulary

Source: `KNM.pdf` uploaded by the user.

## How this file is organised

I first rewrote table rows, arrows, equals signs, and short notes into complete Dutch knowledge-point sentences. Then I translated each knowledge point into correct natural English. Vocabulary is translated by meaning in context, not by a wrong placeholder such as “see the dictionary”.

Each lesson contains about 30 knowledge points. Very small table cells are treated as separate knowledge points when they carry independent exam information. Paragraphs that belong together are kept together.
